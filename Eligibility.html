Laptop for All - Eligibility Criteria

Program Overview

Laptops for All is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing free laptops to deserving students. The eligibility criteria ensure a fair and transparent distribution process.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Enrolled in an accredited degree program
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Academic merit based on a predefined list

Allocation Process

The laptops will be allocated based on a combination of financial need and academic merit. The allocation process will be overseen by a dedicated committee to ensure fairness and transparency.

Application Process

Interested students can apply for the program by filling out the online application form. The committee will review all applications and notify selected students.
